Delete Account & User Data

Request deletion

To permanently delete your ChordCanvas account and all associated data, send an email to brainrotpiano@gmail.com with the subject "Delete my account" and include the email address you used to sign up.

We will process your request within 30 days and confirm when your data has been removed.

What gets deleted

  • Your account (email, username)
  • All saved chord arrangements and library items
  • Subscription and billing records (after any required retention period)
