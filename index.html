Chord Canvas

Chord Canvas is an open source project under the MIT License. Do you like Chord Canvas? 🤗 Buy me a coffee ☕ so I can continue developing more music and art related projects!

My name is Beats Away, a music producer and sound designer. I started this project because I have always wanted to just type in any chords to generate customisable MIDI patterns or quality WAV audio without having to purchase a DAW or subscribe to a sound library.

Special thanks to S. Christian Collins. His GeneralUser GS soundfont project (SF2) is converted into WAV files for providing many of the instruments here.
