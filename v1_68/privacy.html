Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Overview

ChordCanvas ("we", "our") is a music creation app. We collect and use data only to provide and improve the service.

Data we collect

Account data: When you sign in with Google or Instagram, we receive your email address and username/display name from the provider. We store these to identify your account and display your profile.

Usage data: Chord arrangements, saves, and library items you create are stored so you can access them across sessions.

Billing: If you subscribe, payment is handled by Stripe. We do not store your full payment details; we receive subscription status and billing identifiers.

How we use data

We use your data to provide ChordCanvas features (save, load, sync), manage your account and subscription, and improve the app.

Third parties

We use Google and Instagram for sign-in, Stripe for payments, and Cloudflare for hosting. Each has its own privacy policy governing their handling of data.

Data retention

We retain your data while your account exists. You may request deletion at any time.

Contact

For privacy questions or to request deletion, contact brainrotpiano@gmail.com or see our Delete account page.
